Malaria Detection

Deep Learning and Medical Image Analysis

Malaria is still a major global health burden, with more than 3.2 billion people in 91 countries remaining at risk of the disease. Accurately distinguishing malaria from other diseases, especially uncomplicated malaria (UM) from non-malarial infections (nMI), remains a challenge. This project aims to detect and distinguish between infected and non-infected cells using Keras and Python language
